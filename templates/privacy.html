Privacy & Security Policy

1. Data Protection

All tea factory reports, mismatch data, and leaf collector remarks are confidential. Access is restricted to authorized users only.

2. Prohibition of Screen Recording & Screenshots

Strictly prohibited: Any form of screen capture, recording, or photography of the system interface, reports, or data without explicit written permission from KDU Group management.

This system employs technical measures to discourage unauthorized capture, including keyboard shortcut detection and content protection.

4. User Accountability

Each user action is logged. Any violation of this policy will result in immediate account suspension and disciplinary action.

5. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact IT Security: security@kdugroup.com
